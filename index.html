About

Curious by nature, precise by practice.

I'm Alex — a product designer based in Bangalore with seven years of experience shaping digital products across fintech, health, and consumer apps. I obsess over the gap between what a product does and what a user actually needs.

Before going independent, I led design at Zeta and spent two years at Google's APAC office working on emerging-market UX. I've also co-founded two startups — one failed quietly, one failed loudly. Both taught me more than any job.

Outside of work: I read too much, cook just enough, and watch an embarrassing amount of NBA basketball.
