About

Curious by nature, precise by practice.

I'm Neha — a UX designer with four years of experience shaping digital products, mostly in fintech and B2B, with detours into motor, entertainment, and oil & gas along the way. I obsess over the gap between what a product does and what a user actually needs.

I did my post-graduation in Graphic Design from MITID, and naturally found my way to UX design by the end of the course. Since then, I've worked at a UX design consultancy in Mumbai, across both agile and waterfall teams.

What's next? I'm looking to work with a product firm — somewhere I get exposure to data, and the process involves making gradual, measurable changes. New B2B and B2C products excite me immensely. I want to work with a team that challenges and inspires me.
