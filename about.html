About

Hi! I am Nehal.

I have 4+ years of experience as a Product Manager and 12+ years overall.

As a product manager, I am passionate about using my skills to make end users' lives better. I aspire to go deeper and broader into the subject matter at hand, and work with humane organisations that spend resources on asking the right questions and chasing solutions through logical and scientific methods.

I started my career as a writer in the advertising and marketing industry, and very soon moved into user experience design. As a UX design consultant, I primarily worked on banking and fintech transformation. I then switched to a product company and organically grew into a product manager's role. As a product manager, I have primarily worked on 0→1, B2B-SaaS products across 2 different domains — Design Operations and SDLC Observability.
